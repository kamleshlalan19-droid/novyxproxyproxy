Privacy

CanLite is designed to keep your on-site preferences simple and device-based. This page explains what stays local, what happens when you visit outside pages, and how to contact us if you need help.

What stays on your device

Theme choices, favorites, and similar convenience settings are primarily stored in your browser so the site feels familiar the next time you open it.

What happens on third-party pages

When you open a game, tool, or external site, that destination can follow its own rules, cookies, and data practices. Their policies apply once you are using their content.

Account and support requests

If you choose to sign in, contact support, or complete an offerwall flow, the information needed for that feature may be processed so the request works as expected.

Security and common sense

We work to keep the site stable and straightforward, but no website can promise perfect privacy or perfect security across the internet. Use the same judgment you would use anywhere else online.

Questions

If you have a privacy question, need help with a page, or want something reviewed, email canlite24@outlook.com.
