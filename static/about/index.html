About CanLite

CanLite brings together browsing, games, and site links in one place.

What you can do here

The proxy lets you browse in tabs. The games section gives you featured titles, a full library, favorites, and play pages.

How it is organized

Home links to the main sections, Games covers the library, Extras holds additional pages, and Partners links to related sites.

If a link stops working

Check Discord for updated links and quick help. It is the easiest place to find the latest working route.

Contact
